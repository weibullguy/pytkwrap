=====================
pytkwrap Requirements
=====================

The key words **SHALL**, **SHOULD**, **TBD**, and **TBR** are used as
follows throughout the pytkwrap requirements:

- **SHALL** indicates a mandatory requirement that must be satisfied.
- **SHOULD** indicates a recommended requirement that is not strictly
  mandatory but represents good practice.
- **TBD** (To Be Determined) indicates that the content is not yet
  known and must be resolved before the requirement can be considered
  complete. A requirement containing **TBD** is open and cannot be
  verified.
- **TBR** (To Be Resolved) indicates that the content is known but
  contains an open issue or conflict that must be resolved before the
  requirement can be baselined. A requirement containing **TBR** is
  under review and cannot be verified until resolved.
